# Public Presence

Public Presence covers private authoring for talent pages plus visitor-facing public pages and Marshmallow concepts.

## Main Areas

- Talent homepage management.
- Studio, preview, and editor surfaces.
- Asset/page component editing.
- Marshmallow management and moderation entry points.
- Public visitor pages and short links.

## Current Caveat

Public Marshmallow visitor submission and related visitor workflows are not accepted as available procedures while the blocker-level public visitor limitation remains open.

Detailed guide: [Public Presence](../user-guide/public-presence/README.md).
